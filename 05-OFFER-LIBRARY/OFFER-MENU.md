# Offer Library

These are the first productized offers that should be built and sold from this operating system.

## Offer 1 — AI Lead Capture + Qualification Dashboard

### Buyer

- Mortgage brokers
- Insurance agents
- Financial advisors
- Consultants
- Local service businesses

### Problem

Leads arrive through forms, calls, DMs, or referrals but are not qualified, followed up with, or tracked consistently.

### Deliverable

- Landing page
- Lead capture form
- Qualification questions
- Admin dashboard
- Email notification
- CSV export
- Optional CRM webhook

### Pricing

```text
Setup: $3,000 to $7,500
Monthly support: $500 to $1,500
```

Access beats market size.
