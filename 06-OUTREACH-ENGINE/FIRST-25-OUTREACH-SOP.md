# First 25 Outreach SOP

## Purpose

Book the first sales conversations for the selected AI systems offer.

## Target

25 highly relevant businesses where the owner or decision maker can understand the value quickly.

## Prospect Fields

Create a spreadsheet with:

- Business name
- Website
- Owner / decision maker
- Email
- Phone
- LinkedIn
- Specific signal observed
- Pain hypothesis
- Recommended offer
- Personalization hook
- Outreach status
- Follow-up date
- Notes

## Signal Types

Prioritize prospects with visible signs of pain or opportunity:

- Weak website conversion
- No obvious lead capture
- Slow or missing follow-up path
- Poor reviews mentioning responsiveness
- Hiring for admin/sales support
- Active advertising but weak landing page
- Manual intake process
- Recent growth or new location
- New financing, expansion, or partnership
- High-value service with poor online funnel

## Daily Outreach Rhythm

```text
Monday: Build 25 prospect list
Tuesday: Create 5 personalized demo angles
Wednesday: Record/send 5 Looms
Thursday: Follow up and send 5 more
Friday: Book calls and update CRM
```

## Permission-Based First Message

```text
Hey [Name] — quick one for you.

I noticed [specific signal about their business].

I built a short demo showing how [Business Name] could capture and qualify more inquiries automatically before they fall through the cracks.

Would you be open to me sending the 60-second video?

Dennis
```

## Loom Structure

60 seconds maximum:

1. Greet by name
2. Show their website or business context
3. Identify one leak or opportunity
4. Show the demo adapted to them
5. Explain the business outcome
6. Ask for a 15-minute conversation

## Follow-Up 1

```text
Hey [Name], just checking back.

The idea is simple: give your business a cleaner way to capture, qualify, and follow up with inquiries without adding admin work.

Worth sending the short demo?
```

## Follow-Up 2

```text
Last note from me.

I may be wrong, but it looks like [Business Name] could benefit from a more automated intake and follow-up process.

If useful, I can send the demo. If not relevant, no worries.
```

## Call Objective

Do not over-explain the technology.

Learn:

- Current lead flow
- Where leads get lost
- Current tools
- Value of one new customer
- Cost of poor follow-up
- Decision process
- Timeline

Then recommend one clear package.

## Sales Call Close

```text
Based on what you described, I would not start with a complicated AI system.

I would build a focused lead capture and qualification system for [Business Name] that does three things:

1. Captures inquiries cleanly
2. Qualifies them before they hit your calendar
3. Gives you a dashboard and follow-up process

I can have the first version live in [timeline].

The setup is $[price], and ongoing support is $[monthly]/month so you do not have to manage the technical side.

If that works, I can send the agreement and invoice today.
```

## Metrics

Track weekly:

- Prospects researched
- Personalized messages sent
- Looms sent
- Replies
- Calls booked
- Proposals sent
- Deals closed
- Retainers added
