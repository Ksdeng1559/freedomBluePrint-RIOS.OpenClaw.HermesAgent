# Mission Brief Template

## Purpose

Use this template to convert a business goal into a controlled mission that Hermes can route, workers can execute, humans can approve, and RIOS can store for future intelligence.

Every mission should be clear enough that a new operator can understand what is being done, why it matters, who owns it, what evidence is needed, and where approval is required.

## Final Mission Rule

No mission is complete until it has:

1. A business goal
2. A worker assignment
3. Evidence requirements
4. An approval gate
5. A definition of done
6. A decision log entry
